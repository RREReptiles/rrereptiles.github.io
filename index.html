Welcome to Red Rocks Exotic Reptiles!

We sell all sorts of geckos, skinks, arboreal lizards, and other reptile supplies and insect feeders! We also offer aquatic plants, aquarium keeping goods, and our newest edition: entomology art!

We are always looking to expand our breeding efforts and business, so feel free to reach out to us with trade suggestions or just any comments you may have! We're confident you'll have a great experience working with us.

At the moment, we offer Pictus Geckos, Dune Geckos, Cave Geckos, Crested Geckos, and several other species of gecko. We also carry Emerald Tree Skinks, Green Keel-Bellied Lizards, Gidgee skinks, and Dekay's Brown Snakes, amongst many other reptiles. We also carry bug feeders, aquatic plants, shrimp, medication and dry goods — stay tuned for new products!

About Red Rocks Exotic Reptiles

We started out raising reptiles for fun and as a hobby, but as time went on we thought, why not help spread the joy of reptile keeping to others? So, we opened our business in 2023 in the beautiful centennial state of Colorado.

We chose the name Red Rocks Exotic Reptiles because we absolutely love the striking beauty that the Red Rocks state park offers, and we felt that it was a great comparison to the beauty we see in reptiles as well!

We are proud to be a woman, Veteran, Hispanic, and Native American owned company.

The joy we get from seeing pets with their caretakers is part of why we started our business. We want as many people as possible to feel the way we do about animals. We work with our customers to educate and provide them with everything they and their scaled friends need for a great life together.

Our Vision & Mission

🌟 Vision Statement

To revolutionize the reptile industry by setting a new standard for clean, ethical breeding practices, putting the health of the reptiles first, and fostering trust and confidence among reptile enthusiasts worldwide.

🎯 Our Mission

At Red Rocks Exotic Reptiles, our mission is to provide reptile enthusiasts with ethically bred, captive-born and bred (CBB) reptiles that are not only healthy and well-tempered but also ethically sourced. We are committed to maintaining impeccable standards of cleanliness and husbandry, handling our reptiles daily to ensure their socialization and well-being, and fostering a transparent and trustworthy relationship with our customers.

Our Core Values

What this means for YOU, the customer.

⚖️ Ethical Breeding

We are dedicated to practicing ethical breeding methods, caring only for captive-bred and born reptiles to ensure the sustainability of wild populations and the welfare of individual animals. You will only receive healthy and captive bred reptiles from us, guaranteed free of parasites and deformities most wild caught (WC) reptiles exhibit.

💚 Animal Welfare

We prioritize the health, well-being, and temperament of every reptile in our care, providing them with the highest standards of husbandry and enrichment.

👁️ Transparency

We believe in open communication and transparency in all aspects of our business, from our breeding practices to our customer interactions, to build trust and confidence among our clientele. If you have any questions at any time, even post-purchase, we are here to help!

⭐ Customer Satisfaction

We are committed to exceeding customer expectations by providing exceptional service, expert advice, and ongoing support throughout the reptile ownership journey.

📚 Education

We're not just here to run a business — we are also passionate about educating our customers and the public about responsible reptile care, habitat enrichment, and conservation efforts to empower them to be knowledgeable and responsible reptile enthusiasts.

🤝 Community Engagement

We actively engage with and support the reptile and wildlife community through partnerships, donation programs, and educational initiatives to promote responsible reptile ownership and conservation efforts. We also offer photography, videography, and free social media promotions!

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12/6/25

We respect your privacy. We want you to feel confident using our app and website, and we are committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

ReptiLog can be used entirely offline without creating an account. In this mode, no personal data is collected or transmitted, and all information remains stored locally on your device.

If you choose to create an optional account to enable cloud sync features, we collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Authentication information (securely handled by Supabase Auth)
  • Your synced app data (such as reptiles, groups, logs, and settings)

This information is stored securely on our backend provider, Supabase, which acts solely as a data processor on our behalf.

How We Use Your Information

Cloud-synced data is used only to:

  • Provide account access
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Backup your information securely

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase to provide secure authentication and cloud data storage. Supabase does not use your data for any purpose other than providing these services to us.

ReptiLog does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Account Deletion

If you have created an account and wish to delete it along with all associated data, you may request deletion at any time.

To request account deletion, email us at: rrereptiles@gmail.com

We will permanently delete your account and all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and make it available within the app and on our website.
