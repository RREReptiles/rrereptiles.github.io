Welcome to Our Website!

We sell all sorts of geckos, skinks, arboreal lizards, and other reptile supplies and insect feeders! We also offer aquatic plants and aquarium keeping goods. We are always looking to expand our breeding efforts and business, so feel free to reach out to us with trade suggestions or questions!

About Our Business

Formed in Colorado in 2023, what started as a lifelong hobby turned into a full-blown business. We finally decided that we want to help diversify the breeding of particular reptile species.
